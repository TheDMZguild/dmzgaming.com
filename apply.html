Important - Please read before applying

Our application form is hosted by WoWAudit, and will require you to log in using your Battle.net credentials (so have your authenticator ready!). By logging in with your Battle.net account, we will recieve links to your character's armory and Warcraft Logs automatically, so no need to send us any additional links!

Note: WoWAudit is a popular and trusted website used by many guilds, but if you have any questions please don't hesitate to contact an officer using the details to the right of this post.

  1. Read the raider rules below, and ensure you can agree to them before applying.
  2. Click the green button and log in using your Battle.net account.
  3. Select the character that you wish raid on, the role you wish to be considered for, and answer all of the questions before submitting your application.
  4. We will review your application and make a decision witin 1 week (usually sooner).
  5. You can view the status of you application any time using the same link (i.e. click the big green button).
  6. If you are successful, we will contact you via Discord to arrange your invite to the guild in game.
  7. If you have been accepted, but haven't recieved a Discord message, then please reach out to an officer via Discord or via Bnet / in game.

Raider rules

Raid Times
Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday: 21:00 to 23:30 Server Time

Attendance
Raiders are expected to:

  • Sign up for every raid in advance and update their status if it changes, be respectful of your fellow raiders, and don't leave things to the last minute
  • Be online half an hour before the listed start time of each raid for which they have signed up as available. Invites will go in the 15 mins before raid start
  • Be available for a minimum of 2 of the 3 raids a week
  • Come fully repaired and prepared for every raid with enough consumables to cover any eventuality
  • Be familiar with the tactics for every encounter which might conceivably be attempted during the raid (watch videos, read tactics)
  • Have the follow addons as a minimum - DBM/Bigwigs, Weak Auras, and ERT
  • Have their gear appropriately gemmed and enchanted for the role they perform in the raid

Note: If you sign as tentative and dont change this in the last 30 mins pre-raid or give us an update on why you're still unsure about your status you will be considered unavailable.

Officers start to plan the raid from 7 pm and spend most of that time discussing and deciding who will be attending. We already have to rotate raiders out, checking out logs/attendance, etc and it's hard enough, without sign-ups changing at the last minute, so if you want to be considered for selection, make sure those buttons are clicked on the calendar!

Pugging raids
Our rules are clear: Raiding mains must not get themselves saved to any current progression raiding content unless it's an official guild run. Everything else is freely puggable at all times. However, if you have a decent reason for wanting to do so (for instance, you can't attend any raids for the rest of the week, and aren't saved somewhere and would like to try to pug it) please talk to an officer for approval.

Non-raiding mains and alts may pug at will.
