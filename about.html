About us

In 2005, two young(ish) Battlefield 2 players decided to start their own community of like-minded gamers. After investing in several gaming servers and throwing together a simple website, the community quickly grew to over 100 members from across the globe who all shared the same approach to gaming - play hard, have fun, and don't be a dick!

After a brief foray into Battlefield 2142 (yuck!) during 2006, three founding members decided to try out a new game that took them away from their FPS roots - an MMO called World of Warcraft. Duffry, MysticPigeon, and Zulu entered the world of Azeroth in 2006, and The DMZ guild was born; a place where no fighting is allowed and people's behaviour, manners, and kindness to each other are always more important than their skill with a mouse and keyboard!
