Welcome
Welcome to The DMZ - a World of Warcraft guild with a focus on Mythic raiding. Founded during Vanilla WoW back in 2005, we're the oldest raiding guild on Eonar, and can offer a stable and friendly guild that's comprised of people of all shapes and sizes. We like to think of ourselves as an inclusive guild and do not tolerate toxicity or discrimination in any form. We have a diverse membership with men and women from across Europe (the Netherlands, UK, Scandinavia etc.), and even some gnomes too!
Raiding with us
We consider ourselves to be a 'semi-hardcore' raiding guild, so while we expect all raiders to meet a good standard of performance and preparation in our raids, we like to play in a fairly relaxed and friendly atmosphere. All raiders are expected to attend 2 of the 3 raids per week on average, and must come well prepared to all raids, with all gear enchanted, consumables etc., with a knowledge of the bosses / tactics.

Raiding schedule (server time):
  • Wednesday 21:00 - 23:30
  • Friday 21:00 - 23:30
  • Tuesday 21:00 - 23:30
We review logs regularly to assess people's performance, and try to help where we can, but we expect everyone to do the same and dedicate some of their time to self-improvement.

A more detailed description of our Raider Rules can be found here or on our Discord server (which you can join by clicking the Discord icon at the top of the page).
